Why Do You Need A Virtual Assistant?

A virtual assistant (VA) is an independent contractor who provides administrative, creative, or logistical support to a company remotely. VAs are typically freelancers who work from home.


Virtual assistants are most commonly hired for admin work. This includes scheduling meetings, booking travel, organizing calendars, and even answering phones via a connecting line.


Virtual assistants can help organize tasks outside of work, in addition to their standard duties. Booking vacations, organizing a calendar, and purchasing gifts for friends and family are some of the more common personal duties.


Time is an important resource when running a business, and sometimes those menial, everyday tasks can become a barrier to focusing on new opportunities and areas for growth. Having a virtual assistant handle your administrative work will ease your workload significantly, providing more time for you and your team to focus on high-impact projects.
